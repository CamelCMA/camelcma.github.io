## About CMA
`CaeMl.Ai`，代表3個領域:
* Cae = Computer-aided engineering = 電腦輔助工程
* Ml = Machine learning = 機器學習
* Ai = Artificial intelligence = 人工智慧

`CaeMlAi`不好發音，故逕取三字開頭，簡稱本站為`CMA`。
